What's Math More?

Math More is a fun website to train you to properly solve addition and subtraction problems!

Frequently Asked Questions

The letters indicate each level's mode. For instance, the E stands for "easy", the M stands for "medium", and the H stands for "hard". The last letter S stands for "story mode". For these three levels, you'll go through a total of ten questions. At the very end, you'll receive a score and a list of problems you got wrong.

Easy mode uses addition and subtraction problems with just one digit! Medium uses two digit numbers, and hard mode uses three digit numbers.

At Math More, we celebrate the small successes! If you got the answer right at a point in time but changed it later, we'll still mark it as correct! However, you'll see your wrong answer show up in the final list of wrong answers so that you can learn from your mistakes.

Math More provides thorough feedback for you to learn from your mistakes. As a result, your score will take into account your final answers. However, if you hesitated on a problem or went back and changed your answer for it, Math More will list it so that you're aware that you're not very confident on that problem.

Story mode is a way to practice basic addition and subtraction problems in a situation context. As a result, there's a story problem that you'll solve! For this mode, you'll be able to immediately see if your answers is correct or not. You'll still get only ten problems.
